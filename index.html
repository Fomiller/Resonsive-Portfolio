About Me

I am graduate of Texas State University, with a major in “Digital Media Innovation” and minor in "Business Administration", and I am interested in a career in digital marketing, web development and web design. I originally enrolled at Texas Tech University, as a freshman, to study Architecture because I had always been fascinated with design in the environment around me, and at that time my interests were focused on buildings. While my year in the Architecture program taught me a great deal and solidified my interest in all things design I also realized that, ultimately, becoming an Architect was not for me. In addition to my passion for design I also have a natural inclination towards marketing and I began to explore the possibility of combining those two areas within marketing classes at Texas State University. When Texas State University then created the “Digital Media Innovation” major, I knew that it was for me as it combined design with a modern, forward thinking approach towards marketing which I find fascinating. I am proud to consider myself a dedicated and hardworking person who enjoys thinking creatively to embrace new ideas and achieve innovative solutions.
